Airfoil Lift Analysis via Discrete Vortex Method

Academic Project | Fluid Mechanics Course | 2023

Academic project focused on calculating the aerodynamic lift and pressure distribution of a parabolic airfoil , utilizing a panel-based numerical method programmed under potential flow theory.

  • Developed a MATLAB numerical algorithm to implement the Discrete Vortex Method for a cambered airfoil.
  • Modeled the inviscid and irrotational flow field by defining panel geometries and correctly satisfying the Kutta condition.
  • Conducted a convergence study to optimize the panel discretization, determining the ideal number of elements for an accurate lift calculation.
  • Validated the computational model by simulating a flat plate and successfully comparing the results against Katz & Plotkin bibliography.
